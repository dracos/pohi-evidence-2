2.1 The parties shall use all reasonable endeavours to work
together to allow the Roll-out of POCL Infrastructure
Services and the Roll-out of DSS Services to commence before
the starting dates specified in the above table and in the
table in paragraph 3.

2.2 The CONTRACTOR shall provide the AUTHORITIES with a
Functional Specification within one (1) week of execution
hereof. Meeting the dates associated with the Contractual
Milestones is contingent upon agreement of the Functional
Specification being given by the AUTHORITIES by 30th June
1996. To help achieve this and to establish the full impact
at the counter, a prototype of the Trial System shall undergo
controlled usability trials. If agreement cannot be reached
by 30th June 1996 the dates in the table above shall be
