The above case was heard at Newcastle Crown Court on the 4" September 2009.
Counsel attended to prosecute represented by our Agents.

to the Indictment.

The case was adjourned to week commencing 14" December 2009 for Trial. Any
further evidence is to be served by the 14" October 2009.

I understand that a Conference has been arranged with Counsel for the 25"
September 2009.
