Thank you for your letter of the 23rd April enclosing details of the invest-
igations costs.

Mrs. Rudkin attended today's sentencing hearing as Stafford Crown Court
represented by Peter Hampton of Counsel. The case was listed in Court 3
before HHJ Glenn. The Case Officer Mike Wilcox also attended,

For the single count of theft of £43,894-15 Mrs. Rudkin was sentenced to

12 months imprisonment suspended for 2 years. Two requirements were imposed
namely that she performs the maximum of 300 hours unpaid work and submits
to an electronically monitored curfew for 6 months requiring her to be at
home between the hours of 10.00 p.m. and 5,00 p.m. In view of her parlous
