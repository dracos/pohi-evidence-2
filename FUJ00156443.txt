The above named Defendants appeared at Norwich Crown Court on 10" February
2011, Mrs Humphrey attending under threat of being forcibly brought to Court under a

warrant, having initially failed to appear.

Both Defendants pleaded guilty on 11" December 2009, however the calculation of the
total loss was disputed and the case proceeded to a Trial of Issue (Newton Hearing),
which finally came before the Court for hearing on 15" and 16" November 2010, where
only Mr Humphrey was in attendance. As a result of cross examination and
documentation put to Mr Humphrey, the Defence concluded the loss as calculated and
charged on the Indictment was in fact correct and that this was the amount stolen by

the Defendants.
