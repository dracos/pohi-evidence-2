Dear Mike,

Thank you for your letter of today, requesting an extension to the contractual
deadline for completion of your analysis of Acceptance Incident 298. I have
considered this with Bruce McNiven, and Chris French. We are not able to
agree to your request, since there would be insufficient time for Post Office
Counters Limited to review any new information that you provided. I note
that this Acceptance Incident was first raised in early July, following analysis
of the Horizon System Help Desk Log by our programme staff, and that
Pathway have already had more than 5 weeks for investigation and analysis
of the problem.

Post Office Counters Limited could only consider an extension to the
timescale for analysis of this Acceptance Incident in the context of an
adjustment to the overall Acceptance timescales. However, in the meantime,
we would encourage you to continue your efforts towards rectification of this
high severity deficiency in the services.
