I am the person named above and have been employed by the Post Office for approximately 26 years. I am
currently employed as a Contracts Advisor based in Wimbourne, Dorset. Although over recent years my job
title has changed I have been involved in dealing with Subpostmaster contractual issues since September
2001.

I am responsible for a number of Sub Post Office Branches (SPO8) within the Post Office network. The area
that I cover can change from time to time, although on 30 March 2011 the Sub Post Offices that I was
responsible for included those in the Portsmouth (PO) postcode area, which thus included the Rowlands Castle
branch, postcode PO9 6BW. On that date I was contacted by Mr Adam Shaw, Field Support Advisor who
advised me of the result of an audit he was conducting at that branch. In view of the unexplained deficit
identified I precautionary suspended Mrs Lynette Hutchings’ contract for services as postmaster at that branch.

postmaster at Rowlands Castle SPOB. I had previously written to Mrs Hutchings on 06 April 2011 inviting her
to attend an interview on 13 April 2011 but I did not receive any response. I now produce a copy of the letter
dated 14 April 2011, which cites the grounds for the termination, as item NA/1.

As postmaster, Mrs Hutchings was responsible for the general day to day running of the office. All postmasters
on appointment are bound by a contract with Post Office Ltd. I now produce copies of sections 1, 12 and 15 of
