I have been employed by, Royal Mail Group (formally known as the Post Office and
Consignia) since 1985. Post Office Ltd employs me as a Security Manager and my duties
include the investigation of crimes committed against Post Office Ltd by its staff and its
agent's employees. I have held this position for 12 months.

I was present with Stephen BRADSHAW on Friday 6" January 2012 at Fazakerley Post
Office branch 404 Longmoor Road Liverpool L9 9DB. An audit was taking place and I
witnessed Anne NIELD hand to Stephen BRADSHAW a letter that she said had been
prepared by both her and Mrs Angela SEFTON. In the letter they stated they had
suppressed Giro deposits from customers.

I can confirm that 40 giro deposit slips and a number of cheque envelopes were recovered

from a cupboard in the counter secure area.
