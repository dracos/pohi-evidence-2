Further to your query.

The position is that POL acts as a private prosecutor in relation to the specific criminal case -
adhering to the Code for Crown Prosecutors and all statutory or other rules in relation to
prosecuting offenders. It should be noted that legislation gives the Director of Public Prosecutions
to right to take over and close down a case if they regard that as appropriate.
