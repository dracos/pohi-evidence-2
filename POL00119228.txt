SUBJECT: - Investigation Ref: - POLTD/0809/0103 - EASINGTON / MARISSA FINN

Following successful negotiations between the FIU, the investigator and the Solicitor representing the
defendant, the full amount of the loss, (27,407.43) was repaid via a BACS payment into a bank account
of Post Office Ltd.

This followed a number of enquiries being undertaken by myself into the properties and potential assets
linked to the offender. However, as the likelihood of a full recovery appeared to be achievable, no further
action was undertaken pending the money being repaid.

A full recovery now having been made the case file is now forwarded for formal closure.
